Main features of the car

The most spectacular expression of AMG Performance

Race-proven AMG suspension and powertrain technologies become part of the most iconic of all SUVs. The unimaginable and the fantastic become true in the Mercedes-AMG G 63. With impressive power from the V8 Biturbo engine, a superb chassis and striking AMG design, the new Mercedes-AMG G 63 will not leave you indifferent.

Exterior design

Spectacular and sporty exterior design of the Mercedes-AMG G 63

The supercharged Mercedes-AMG G 63 SUV exudes power at first glance: the signature AMG radiator grille, wheels up to 22 inches and the impressive AMG Performance exhaust system with two chrome-plated twin tailpipes on the sides – all this makes the exterior extremely striking and extroverted.

AMG styling

Many design details of the Mercedes-AMG G 63 feature exciting AMG styling: the front bumper and radiator grille, the light-alloy wheels and wheel arch extensions, the decorative inlay on the body protection elements, as well as the red-painted AMG brake callipers. A particular highlight are the striking side pipes of the AMG exhaust system.

AMG light alloy wheels

If the Mercedes-AMG G 63 is a high-class athlete, then AMG light-alloy wheels are its shoes. They are of the highest quality and are ready for any load. Optionally, a whole range of alloy wheels up to 22 inches is available, in various design and color options.

AMG RIDE CONTROL suspension

The AMG RIDE CONTROL suspension is your key to superior performance in everyday life in the city and off-road. Adaptive damping offers a wide choice between comfortable and sporty suspension settings. You can choose between Comfort, Sport and Sport+ modes and change the suspension characteristics at any time by pressing a button.

Interior desighn

Mercedes-AMG G 63 More space to unleash significant power

The Mercedes-AMG G 63 has more space inside than ever before in the G-Class's 40-year history. The interior is designed in the best possible conditions so that you can enjoy AMG Driving Performance with all your senses. Exclusive leather upholstery, carbon materials, DINAMICA microfibre – the interior perfectly visualizes what is commonly called “sporty luxury”.

AMG Performance steering wheel

At maximum power, it is important to ensure maximum control. This is easy with the AMG Performance multifunction sports steering wheel: it is made of carbon fiber with DINAMICA microfibre in the grip areas. On the sides of the steering wheel there are aluminum paddle shifters: they allow you to change your behavior on the track at lightning speed.

Seats in signature AMG design

AMG power and dynamics can be so comfortable. Thanks to special AMG sports seats in nappa leather. Their exclusive design and AMG emblem indicate membership in the elite Mercedes-AMG sports club.

AMG decorative elements made of carbon fiber

Carbon fiber decorative elements give the interior of the Mercedes-AMG G 63 a special exclusivity. Carbon fiber blends harmoniously into the interior of the iconic SUV and creates exciting contrasts. They enhance and highlight the original design of the model.
